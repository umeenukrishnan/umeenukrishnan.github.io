I am Meenu 😊

A researcher who loves to code.

I joined the Computational Mechanics Lab in January 2019, and since then, my work has primarily focused on the phase field method for fracture analysis and topology optimization. My work has involved extensive use of open-source software such as FEniCS for finite element analysis, which has greatly facilitated the implementation and testing of our models.

To have a practical application of these computationally efficient algorithms, I work through Avkalan Labs. In addition to this, I share my learnings through EigenPlus.
